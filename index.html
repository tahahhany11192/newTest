Why sharing threat intel is hard

Organizations hesitate to share Indicators of Compromise due to limited trust, fear of data leakage, and unverifiable sources. Information ends up siloed, slowing collective defense.

Blockchain-backed solution

Our smart contracts enable anonymous submissions and public verification. Every IoC is timestamped, signed, and stored as an immutable on-chain record.

  • Blockchain

    Decentralized and tamper-proof ledger for all threat data

  • Anonymity

    Contributors remain private while sharing valuable intelligence

  • Integrity

    Data cannot be tampered with after submission

  • Crowdsourced

    Global community of security professionals sharing intelligence
